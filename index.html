About Us

Dee Three Learning Academy focuses on personalized, creative, and meaningful education experiences. We blend academic tutoring, global languages, and artistic development in a nurturing environment tailored for primary school children.

Our Programmes

Artistic Development

From Phonics to Drawing, Zumba to Bharatanatyam, nurturing every child's creativity.

Global Language Classes

Spoken English, Hindi, Tamil, and Arabic lessons with fun and interaction.

Academic Support

Home tuition designed for primary school learners, blending support and encouragement.

Vision

We envision empowering students to become thoughtful, creative, and self-assured individuals, fully equipped to contribute positively to their communities. We are committed to fostering a nurturing environment where students can truly uncover and develop their true potential.

Mission

To deliver exceptional educational experiences, developing critical thinking, and rigorously cultivate the unique talents and abilities of every student. We are dedicated to establishing a supportive and inclusive learning community that motivates students to pursue excellence, have meaningful connections, and bring positive change.
